Day 21 — After-Action Review & Improvement Plan

Goal: Analyze the full Day 1–20 Windows hacking bootcamp, evaluate red and blue team performance, identify skill gains and gaps, and create a roadmap for future advanced scenarios.

Step 1 — Campaign Debrief Collection

  • Gather all logs, screenshots, BloodHound data, and notes from Days 1–20.
  • Compile red team attack chains, blue team detection points, and missed indicators.
  • Ensure both sides share full details of tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used.

Step 2 — Performance Metrics Review

  • Measure: Red team success rate in achieving objectives, Blue team detection rate and mean time to detect (MTTD), Blue team mean time to respond (MTTR) and contain (MTTC), Number of false positives vs. true positives.
  • Compare to Day 1 baseline capabilities.

Step 3 — Skill Gap Identification

  • Red team: Identify areas where attacks were easily detected or failed.
  • Blue team: Identify undetected activities and delayed responses.
  • Prioritize gaps that had the largest operational impact.

Step 4 — Detection and Defense Updates

  • Based on findings:
    • Expand Sysmon and PowerShell logging coverage.
    • Adjust correlation rules for stealth techniques.
    • Strengthen incident response workflows.
  • Document new detection logic and distribute to the team.

Step 5 — Offensive Strategy Refinement

  • Develop new attack variations to bypass updated defenses.
  • Add novel persistence, escalation, and evasion methods to the red team playbook.
  • Plan for integrating real-world adversary TTPs from threat intelligence sources.

Step 6 — Roadmap for Next Training Cycle

  • Schedule next month's advanced simulation series.
  • Include: More complex multi-stage intrusions, Larger simulated enterprise environments, Integration of cloud/hybrid infrastructure into attack/defense scenarios.

End of Day 21 Success Criteria:

✅Comprehensive after-action report completed.
✅Metrics and skill gap analysis documented.
✅Updated detection and attack playbooks ready.
✅Clear roadmap created for future advanced training cycles.
