Day 19 — Offensive Persistence Re-Entry

Goal: Simulate a red team attempt to re-establish persistence in lab.local immediately after the blue team's Day 18 persistence hunt, testing whether updated defenses and detection rules can identify and block new, more creative footholds.

Step 1 — Review Blue Team Updates

  • Analyze detection rules and removal processes implemented on Day 18.
  • Identify blind spots or areas where persistence hunting coverage is weak.
  • Plan new persistence techniques that exploit these gaps.

Step 2 — Select Advanced Persistence Methods

  • Use less common techniques that are harder to detect:
    • Modifying legitimate scheduled tasks instead of creating new ones.
    • Leveraging obscure registry keys (e.g., HKCU\Environment variables).
    • Creating WMI event subscriptions with rare triggers.
    • Hiding payloads within legitimate binaries (living-off-the-land binaries).
    • Establishing GPO changes with minimal modifications to existing scripts.

Step 3 — Deploy Persistence Stealthily

  • Avoid high-noise actions that would trigger alerts from Day 18 tuning.
  • Spread deployments across multiple machines to increase survivability.
  • Randomize timing of persistence activation to avoid immediate detection.

Step 4 — Monitor Blue Team Response

  • As the red team, observe whether:
    • Persistence is detected through Sysmon, WEF, or PowerShell logging.
    • Alerts are generated in near real time.
    • Blue team isolates compromised machines or accounts promptly.

Step 5 — Blue Team Actions

  • Hunt for:
    • Modifications to existing scheduled tasks.
    • Registry changes outside previously monitored keys.
    • WMI filters/consumers with uncommon event queries.
    • Slight alterations to legitimate logon scripts.
  • Validate detection of persistence activation events.

Step 6 — Debrief and Adjust

  • Red team: Document persistence methods used, stealth measures applied, and which remained undetected.
  • Blue team: Identify which new techniques bypassed detection and update hunting/detection coverage.
  • Jointly define countermeasures to block future re-entry attempts.

End of Day 19 Success Criteria:

✅Red team deployed at least two creative persistence methods
✅Blue team detected and removed as many as possible
✅Detection rules expanded to cover newly tested techniques
